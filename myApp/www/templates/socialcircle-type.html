We have two options available.

1. Singular - For this type of circle, the pooled money goes to the creator of the group. No transaction fees apply to the creator but the fees is split equally by the remaining members. This type group runs infinitely and can be cancelled anytime.


2. Regular - This is a regular type of circle, the pooled money goes around to each member of the group. The transaction fees is equally split among members of the group. This type of group ends when all the members in the group get paid.
